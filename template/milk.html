Its high-fat content not only enhances the taste but also makes it ideal for making homemade ghee, traditional desserts, and a creamier curd. Perfect for indulgence, Country Delight buffalo milk is a testament to our promise of delivering milk in its purest form with minimal processing.


Cow Milk:

Sweet and aromatic Our cow milk has a naturally sweet taste and shows a hint of yellowish hue when boiled. Both are signs of purity and quality. Carefully sourced from healthy cows grazing on natural fodder, this milk offers a wholesome experience - from a fresh aroma to a satisfying mouthfeel. Its naturally occurring vitamins make pure cow milk an excellent choice for everyday consumption, both adults and kids alike.

Low Malai Milk:

Sourced with care, it promises not only ease of digestion but also the inherent benefits of cow milk.
